# what is statistics?

Statistics is the branch of math/data science that deals with collecting, organizing, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data to make decisions or conclusions.

**Key points**

Uses: summarizing data, finding patterns/trends, comparing groups, making predictions/decisions under uncertainty, and testing hypotheses.

**Types**

Descriptive statistics: summarizes data (mean/median/mode, charts, standard deviation)
Inferential statistics: uses sample data to draw conclusions about a larger population (hypothesis testing, confidence intervals)
