# What is Google Business

Google Business is a free tool from Google that lets businesses manage their online presence across Google Search and Google Maps. It helps customers find business information such as location, hours, phone number, website, and reviews.

## How to list your first business on Google

1. Go to the Google Business Profile website: https://www.google.com/business
2. Sign in with your Google account or create one.
3. Click "Manage now".
4. Enter your business name.
5. Select the business category that best describes your business.
6. Add the business location if you have a physical store or service area.
7. Enter contact details like phone number and website.
8. Choose whether you serve customers outside your location.
9. Verify your business using the verification method offered (usually postcard, phone, or email).
10. After verification, complete your profile with photos, business hours, services, and a description.

Once your profile is verified and complete, your business can appear on Google Search and Google Maps.
